Co-curiiicular Activities



The increasing tuition menace has greatly affected the active participation of students in Co-curricular and Extra Curricular Activities. They are more keen to attend as many tuition classes as possible in their free time than to get involved in these Activities. It is almost impossible to get them for any activity after school or during weekends and therefore we are not able to maintain even sports and games to a high standard.
