COLLEGE IDENTITY

 PRINCIPALS 1854 – 2004 

LIST OF PRINCIPALS & ADMINISTRATIVE HEADS OVER THE 150 YEAR PERIOD


1854 – 1856

REV. FR. FELICE ZOPPI O.S.F. (Founder)


1856 – 1869

Under the supervision of Parish Priests, the school had a succession of laymen who functioned as 'Administrative Heads' during this period. They were;
M/s. PAUL POOREY, F. PEIRIS, F. DE SILVA, K.A. FERNANDO, A. STAPLES, A.B.GEDDES & O.D. PAUL


1870

IRISH CHRISTIAN REV. BROS. JOHN & PAUL


1871 – 1872 

MR. W. HOPP (Administrative Head)


1872 – 1874

MR. J. JORDEN (Administrative Head)


1875 – 1876 

REV. FR. D.H. VANDERSTRAATEN O.S.B.


1876 – 1877

REV. FR. PAUL PERERA O.S.B.


1877 – 1879

REV. FR. D.M. CRANER O.S.B.


1880 – 1892

MR. R.P. JANSZ (Head Master)


1892 – 1898

REV. FR. D.H. LEITAN O.S.B.


1898 – 1906

REV. FR. D.M. CRANER O.S.B.


1907 – 1914 

REV. FR. D. PHILIP CASPERSZ O.S.B.


1915

REV. FR. D. BASIL HYDE O.S.B.


1916 – 1921

REV. FR. JAMES CASPERSZ O.S.B.


1921 – 1943

REV. FR. D. LAWRENCE HYDE O.S.B.


1944 – 1957

REV. FR. D. ROSATI O.S.B.


1957 – 1961

REV. FR. HILARION RUDOLPH O.S.B.


1961 – 1967 

REV. FR. D.I. ROBINSON O.S.B.


1968 – 1977

REV. FR. D. AIDAN DE SILVA O.S.B.

REV. FR. D. LANFRANC AMERASINGHE O.S.B.

REV. FR. STEPHEN ABRAHAM O.S.B.


1994 – 2005

REV. FR. HILARION FERNANDO O.S.B.

REV. FR. D.H. VANDERSTRAATAN OSB

1875-1876

Born in 1840. Ordained in 1866, at the Lateran Basilica in Rome.Died on 16 May 1879 in Pitipana, Negombo. 

REV. FR. D. HILARION LEITAN OSB

1892-1898

Ordained on 19th December 1891. Studied at Royal College, Colombo. Died on 19th March 1933.

REV. FR. D. MAURUS CRANER OSB
1898-1906

Born 25th February 1856. Studied at St. Anthony's College, Kandy. First Old Boy Principal. Ordained on 26th December 1879. Died 19th January 1917.

REV. FR. LAWRENCE HYDE OSB
1921-1943

Born in India, on 8th November 1891. Ordained on 22nd December 1917. The longest serving Principal of St. Anthony's College. Died on 21st December 1982.

REV. FR. D. ANGELO ROSATI OSB
1944-1957

Died on 11th September 1957, within a week of seeing his masterpiece 'The College Hall' completed and opened.

REV. FR. D. HILARION RUDOLPH OSB
1957-1961

A graduate from the University of Oxford.

REV. FR. D. I. ROBINSON OSB
1961-1967

Born 9th November 1924. Ordained on 10th February 1950. Eldest Son of the illustrious and distinguished Old Antonian - Charles Robinson, who was the Founder Principal of St. Thomas' College, Matale. Died on 13th November 2001.

REV. FR. D. AIDAN DE SILVA OSB
1968-1977


Born 11th July 1917. Ordained on 25th August 1945. Studied at St. Anthony's College, Kandy. Died on 12th September 1984.

REV. FR. D.L. AMARASINGHE
1977-1979

Born on 25th August 1923. Ordained on 10th February 1950. Studied at St. Anthony's College, Kandy. Also served as a teacher, Warden of Hostels and Director Junior Seminary, since 1955. Has continued to reside at the College premises after the recent retirement, making himself the longest serving priest at St. Anthony's College.

REV. FR. D. STEPHEN ABRAHAM OSB
1979-1994

Born 15th February 1933. Ordained on 17th December 1964.
Studied at St. Joseph's College- Colombo. Also served as Teacher, Prefect of Games, Warden of Hostels and Head Master/Director of Primary Section, at St. Anthony's' College, since 1965.
Address : c/o 130, De La Salle Street, Mutwal 01500 Colombo, Sri Lanka
