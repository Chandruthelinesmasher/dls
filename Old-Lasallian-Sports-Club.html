Old  Lasallians  Sports Club



Rev Bro Hermenegild Joseph inaugurated the Lasallians Sports Club on July 19th  1954,  in the presence of about 100 Old Boys. Most of those original members of the Union have now gone for their reward, but during these 49 years, the Club has extended its fullest co-operation to the College authorities, true to its motto: to promote fellowship among the Old Boys of De La Salle College, and to contribute to the encouragement of studies, games, etc; among the present boys. 


CHEER!   CHEER!


Cheer, cheer and courage display

All you Lasallians join in the fray

Send a volley of cheers on high

Shake down the thunder from the sky

What through the odds be great or small

Dear old La sale will win over all

While her loyal sons are marching

Onward to victory!


Rise boys and loudly proclaim

That ye will never sully her name

Let the hills and woodlands ring

Lift up your hearts and loudly sing

What thought the way be rough or steep

We unto virtue summons will keep

And if e’er our country needs us

Loyal and true we’ll be!
