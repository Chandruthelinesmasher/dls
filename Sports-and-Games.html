COLLEGE SPORTS & ACTIVITIES

Sports & Games


Athletics


  • Champion of the Under 19 Softball Cricket Tournament of Colombo North Schools and Runners-up at Colombo Zonal Competitions.


  • Runners – up at Colombo North Inter Schools Athletic Meet.


  • Mas. Pasindu Ravishka was placed First in the Long Jump Event of Under 11 at the Inter Schools Zonal Athletic Competitions.


Soccer 


  • Our Soccer Teams both Junior and Senior took part in Colombo District Schools Tournament and Milo Trophy Schools Tournament.



Basketball


  • Under the vibrant and dedicated guidance of  Mr. Rex Clementine, a loyal Old Boy, our basketball teams are attempting to reach higher standards and  regain past glories in this game.


  • Mas. H.M.Umesh Prasad was selected to represent the Under 19 Sri Lankan Basketball Team to participate in the Inter-Religious Peace Sports Festival 2007 in South Korea.


  • Bro. Joseph Jeyakanthan, Master-in-Charge of Basketball, was selected by the Sri Lanka Schools Basketball Federation to be the Head of the Delegation of the Under 19 Boy’s and Girls Basketball National Teams to take part in the 4th Asian Schools Basketball Championship – 2007 in  Nah Trang City, Vietnam.
